About Mumbai Indians

Mumbai Indians (MI) is one of the most successful and popular franchises in the Indian Premier League (IPL). Owned by Reliance Industries, MI has won the IPL title a record 5 times (2013, 2015, 2017, 2019, 2020). Known for its strong team spirit and talented players like Rohit Sharma, Jasprit Bumrah, Hardik Pandya, and many more, MI has built a loyal fan base worldwide.

The team plays its home matches at the iconic Wankhede Stadium in Mumbai. MI is known for its resilience, thrilling matches, and a rich legacy in the world of cricket.
