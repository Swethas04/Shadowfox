About Bumrah

Jasprit Bumrah is the cornerstone of Mumbai Indians' bowling attack. Famous for his toe-crushing yorkers and calmness under pressure, Bumrah made a mark from his debut season. Rising through MI's system, he has become one of the best fast bowlers in world cricket. His unique action, pinpoint accuracy, and consistent performances make him nearly unplayable at the death. Bumrah’s growth with MI symbolizes the franchise’s vision of nurturing world-class talents.
