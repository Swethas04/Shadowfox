Dynamic all-rounder and current captain of Mumbai Indians.

  • Role: All Rounder
  • Born: October 11, 1993
  • Batting Style: Right-handed
  • Bowling Style: Right-arm fast-medium
  • IPL Debut: 2015

About Hardik

Hardik Pandya is one of the most impactful all-rounders in Indian cricket. Known for his bold style and explosive batting, he started his IPL career with MI in 2015. He soon rose to fame for his match-finishing abilities and on-field aggression. After captaining Gujarat Titans to a title win, he returned to MI in 2024 as captain. Off the field, he is admired for his fashion, confidence, and perseverance.
