About Arjun

Arjun Tendulkar, son of the iconic Sachin Tendulkar, is stepping into his own as a promising left-arm pacer. While his surname brings expectations, Arjun has impressed MI with his work ethic, discipline, and ability to swing the new ball. He continues to grow with each opportunity and is working hard to make a name on his own terms.
