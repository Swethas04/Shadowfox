About Karn

Karn Sharma is a composed and steady bowler, known for his ability to contain runs and strike at key moments. His performances in domestic cricket have earned him a spot in the MI squad. With a level-headed attitude and consistent line-length, he adds value in the middle overs and provides MI with solid backup options.
