About Naman

Naman Dhir grabbed attention with a powerful debut for Mumbai Indians, showing fearlessness with the bat and composure beyond his age. As an all-rounder, he offers balance and depth to the squad. MI has shown faith in his finishing ability and part-time spin, seeing him as a potential future mainstay.
