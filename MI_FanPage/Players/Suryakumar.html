About Suryakumar

Suryakumar Yadav, or SKY as he’s fondly called, is a dynamic top-order batsman known for his unorthodox shots and match-winning knocks. Starting as a promising youngster, he reinvented himself at MI, becoming a consistent run-scorer and later earning a place in the Indian team. Whether it’s anchoring the innings or accelerating with 360° shots, Surya is the go-to man. His calm attitude, maturity, and game awareness make him a pillar in the MI batting lineup.
