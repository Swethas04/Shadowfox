Mitchell Santner

World-class left-arm spinner and handy lower-order batsman from New Zealand.

  • Role: All Rounder
  • Born: February 5, 1992
  • Batting Style: Left-handed
  • Bowling Style: Left-arm orthodox spin
  • IPL Debut: 2019

About Santner

Mitchell Santner is a reliable all-rounder who has represented New Zealand in all formats. He brings experience and calmness to the MI squad. Known for his accurate spin bowling and intelligent variations, Santner is a go-to bowler in the middle overs. He also offers depth in batting, making him a strong T20 package.
